# Fingo Gizlilik Politikası

**Son güncelleme:** Temmuz 2026

Fingo ("uygulama") olarak kullanıcılarımızın gizliliğine önem veriyoruz. Bu politika, uygulamamızı kullanırken hangi verileri topladığımızı, nasıl kullandığımızı ve nasıl koruduğumuzu açıklar.

---

## 1. Topladığımız Veriler

### Hesap Bilgileri
- E-posta adresi (kayıt ve giriş için)
- İsim, yaş, cinsiyet, mahalle (profil için, isteğe bağlı)
- Profil fotoğrafı ve galeri fotoğrafları (isteğe bağlı)

### Konum Bilgisi
- Uygulama açıkken yakındaki ilanları göstermek için anlık konum kullanılır.
- Konumunuz sunucularımızda saklanmaz; yalnızca ilan oluştururken seçtiğiniz mekan koordinatı kaydedilir.

### İlan ve Mesaj Verileri
- Oluşturduğunuz ilanlar (mekan adı, başlık, açıklama)
- Diğer kullanıcılarla yaptığınız mesajlaşmalar

### Cihaz Bilgisi
- Push bildirimi göndermek için Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token'ı

---

## 2. Verileri Nasıl Kullanıyoruz

- Yakınındaki ilanları haritada göstermek
- Kullanıcılar arasında eşleşme ve mesajlaşma sağlamak
- İlan süresi dolduğunda bildirim göndermek
- Uygulama güvenliğini ve içerik moderasyonunu sağlamak

### Eşleşme ve Mesajlaşma Kuralları

- Bir kullanıcı sizi eşleşmeden çıkardığında, kullanıcı güvenliği gereği o kullanıcının ilanlarına tekrar mesaj gönderemezsiniz. Bu kural geçmiş konuşma kayıtları üzerinden otomatik uygulanır.

### İçerik Kuralları ve Moderasyon

- **Fotoğraf kısıtlaması:** Kullanıcılar yalnızca kendilerine ait profil ve galeri fotoğrafı yükleyebilir. Başka kişilere (ör. ilanda aradığınız kişi) veya mekanlara ait fotoğraf yüklemek yasaktır; uygulama bu tür yüklemelere teknik olarak izin vermez.
- **Sohbet içi tek seferlik fotoğraf:** Karşılıklı eşleşme gerçekleştikten sonra, sohbet ekranından kamera veya galeriden tek seferlik görüntülenmek üzere fotoğraf gönderilebilir. Gönderilen fotoğraf, alıcı tarafından açılıp görüntülendiği anda erişime kapatılır ve kısa süre içinde sunucularımızdan kalıcı olarak silinir; bir daha kimse tarafından görüntülenemez. Fotoğrafın kameradan mı galeriden mi gönderildiği, görüntülenirken karşı tarafa gösterilir. Ekran görüntüsü alınması teknik olarak engellenmez; ancak bir kullanıcı ekran görüntüsü aldığında karşı taraf bilgilendirilir. **İstisna:** bir fotoğraf karşı taraf tarafından bildirilirse, o fotoğraf kanıt olarak moderasyon amacıyla saklanır ve otomatik silme uygulanmaz.
- **İlan sınırları:** Bir kullanıcı bir günde en fazla 3 ilan açabilir ve aynı anda en fazla 10 aktif ilana sahip olabilir. Bu sınırlar spam ve kötüye kullanımı önlemek için sunucu tarafında uygulanır.
- **Şikayet mekanizması:** Her kullanıcı, uygunsuz bulduğu bir ilanı gerekçe seçerek bildirebilir. Farklı kullanıcılardan gelen bildirimler eşiği aştığında ilan otomatik olarak gizlenir ve sahibine geçici ilan verme kısıtlaması uygulanır. Bildirimler kötüye kullanıma karşı saatlik ve günlük kotalarla sınırlandırılmıştır.
- İlan başlığı ve açıklamaları; hakaret, kişisel bilgi paylaşımı ve benzeri ihlallere karşı otomatik olarak denetlenir.

# Privacy Policy — Fingo

**Effective date:** 2026-06-18
**Developer:** Taha Ercan (fingosupport@gmail.com)

Fingo is an anonymous, location-based app that helps you find someone you
saw, a lost dog, or a face you remember, through posts tied to a real
venue. Until you "match," your identity is hidden as a swan; after a match,
your real name and profile are revealed only to the matched person.

This document explains what data Fingo collects, why, and how it is used.

---

## 1) Data we collect

### Account information (via Firebase Auth)
- Email address
- Password (only Firebase holds a hashed version; never stored on our servers)
- Display name and gender (used to derive your swan identity)
- Optional: age, neighborhood, bio, profile photo

### Location
- Your **live** location is used only to display nearby venue posts on the
  map.
- Your location is **not stored** on our server. When you create a post,
  only the venue's coordinate (not yours) is saved with the post.
- We do not retain real-time or historical location history of users and
  do not perform continuous background tracking.

### Your posts and messages
- Title, story, venue name and coordinate of posts you create
- Conversations with people you matched with (these are **not** end-to-end
  encrypted — they can be accessed if legally required)
- Post view counts

### One-time chat photos
- Once you have mutually matched with someone, you can send photos in
  chat that are meant to be viewed only once.
- A sent photo becomes inaccessible the moment the recipient opens and
  views it, and is **permanently deleted** from our servers shortly after;
  it can never be viewed again by anyone.
- Whether the photo was sent from the camera or the gallery is shown to
  the recipient (labeled "Live" / "Gallery") when they view it.
- Fingo does **not** technically prevent screenshots (no app can fully
  guarantee this). Instead, if a user takes a screenshot during the chat,
  the other person is notified.
- You are still expected to respect the privacy of third parties (including
  other people who may appear in the photo) when using this feature.

### Venue photos (optional contribution)
- If you upload a venue background photo, the photo, who uploaded it, and
  the venue coordinate are stored.
- Uploaded venue photos are **public**. Do not upload personal,
  inappropriate, or privacy-violating content.
- **Fingo is not designed for sharing photos of people you are looking
  for.** Do not upload face photos or any imagery that can identify
  individuals without their explicit consent. Such content may be removed
  and the responsible account may be suspended.

### Device / usage data
- App version, Android version, anonymous usage counters (e.g. crash points)
- We do **NOT** collect advertising identifiers. No third-party ad SDK is
  used.

---

## 2) User-generated content and third parties

Fingo is a platform where users can create anonymous posts to find other
people (e.g. someone they saw) or lost animals. Therefore:

- Posts created by a user may contain physical descriptions, situational
  stories or recall-cue information about third parties. **Users are
  solely responsible for the content they share** and agree not to post
  content that violates the privacy of others, that is defamatory,
  discriminatory, or otherwise unlawful.
- Fingo **is not designed for stalking, harassment, threats, harvesting
  personal information, or locating people against their will.** Fingo
  cannot be used to identify individuals, obtain personal data about
  them, or track them without consent. Users may only communicate with
  each other within the mutual-consent matching mechanism. Such misuse
  may result in permanent account closure and, where required, reporting
  to the relevant authorities.
- If you believe you appear in a post or have found content about
  yourself, please contact `fingosupport@gmail.com`; the content will be
  reviewed and removed if necessary.

### Content moderation and user reporting

- Every published post passes through **automated content checks**:
  insults/profanity, personal information leakage (phone, IBAN, social
  media handle, email, etc.), venue-review patterns, and sensitive venue
  types (schools, healthcare, government, religious, residential). These
  checks are keyword/regex based; no data is sent to any AI model.
- Users can **report** other users' posts (5 reasons: venue review,
  insult, spam, personal info, other). A user may report a given post
  only once; hourly and daily report quotas guard against spam.
- If **3 different users** report a post, the post is **automatically
  hidden** and a **7-day listing-creation ban** is applied to the owner.
  Repeated violations may result in permanent account suspension.
- Auto-hidden posts are reviewed by the Fingo team; an unjust takedown
  can be reversed. Appeals: `fingosupport@gmail.com`.

---

## 3) Anonymity and matching

- **Before a match is confirmed**, users' real names and profile details
  are not shown to other users. The other side sees only a randomly
  generated swan identity (e.g. "Female Swan #0421") and a gender icon.
- **After a match is confirmed**, each party's name, age, neighborhood,
  bio and profile photo become mutually visible.
- Either party may end the match at any time; once ended, messaging in
  that conversation is blocked.

---

## 4) Why we collect

- To provide the service (showing posts and enabling messaging)
- Security (photo reporting / auto-hide / banning)
- Legal obligations when lawfully required

---

## 5) Who we share data with

- **Firebase (Google LLC):** authentication, anonymous analytics, push
  notifications (FCM)
- **Supabase (Frankfurt, EU):** database hosting (PostgreSQL)
- **Cloudflare R2 (EU/US):** storage of user-uploaded profile and venue
  photos
- **Google Cloud:** backend API hosting
- **Yandex (Yandex N.V.):** map rendering. Your location may appear in
  Yandex's internal logs; we do not apply additional masking on the device.
- Data is **never sold** and **not shared with third parties for
  advertising**.
- Data may be processed on servers of service providers located in the
  European Union and/or other countries. International transfers are
  carried out under the standard contractual clauses and data-protection
  safeguards of the relevant provider.
- **Sharing with legal authorities:** Other than the infrastructure
  providers listed above, your personal data is **never shared with anyone
  else** — except when a competent legal authority (a court, prosecutor, or
  law enforcement agency) makes a valid request based on applicable law,
  in which case only the specifically requested data is shared. Absent
  such a request, your data is never shared with third parties.

---

## 6) Retention

- While account is active: indefinite
- If you **disable** your account: data is retained, swan identity is
  hidden from others
- If you **permanently delete** your account: user row, profile photo and
  posts are deleted within 30 days. Conversations with matched users are
  anonymized ("Deleted user") upon their request.

---

## 7) Your rights (GDPR / KVKK)

- Request a copy of your data
- Request deletion — two ways:
  - **In-app:** Profile → Settings → Account Settings → Permanently Delete Account
  - **Without opening the app:** email `fingosupport@gmail.com` with your
    account email and the words "I want to delete my account"
  - Either way, the deletion request enters a 30-day grace period; logging
    in again during that period automatically cancels the request. After
    30 days your account, posts, photos and messages are permanently
    deleted. A minimal record containing no personal data (when the
    account existed and when it was deleted) is kept solely to respond to
    lawful data requests.
- Object to processing: fingosupport@gmail.com
